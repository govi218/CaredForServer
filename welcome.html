Welcome to Caredfor!

 

Before you receive your first care report, you’ll need to complete the following steps to get started:

 

  1. Download the Caredfor app available in the Google Play store
  2. Create a relative account using your email or Google account
  3. Scan the unique QR code below assigned to your loved one
  4. After scanning, our system will link your Caredfor account to your loved one’s profile

{{{img_ref}}}

Once verified, your email will also be linked to receive care notifications input by your loved one’s caregiver. You’ll receive this information once the caregiver has completed their visit, with the relevant care information such as Physical Activity, Mood & Wellbeing, Medication, Food Intake, Hygiene, Caregiver Comments, Visitation Schedule, and Health Checkups.

 

Please note, you may not receive an update for all care tasks each day as some activities may differ day-to-day.

 

If you have any questions or trouble scanning the QR code, please email us at info@caredforapp.ca

 

We hope you enjoy your experience!
